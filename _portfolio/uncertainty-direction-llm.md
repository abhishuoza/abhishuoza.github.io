## Overview

This project investigated whether uncertainty can be represented as a linear direction in large language model (LLM) activation space. Explores the geometry of model internals to understand how LLMs represent confidence and uncertainty in their predictions.

**Tech Stack:** Python, PyTorch, Transformers
