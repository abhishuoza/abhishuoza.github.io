## Overview

Training Qwen2.5-1.5B-Instruct on math reasoning using Group Relative Policy Optimization (GRPO) applied with QLoRA on a single consumer GPU. Demonstrates that advanced reinforcement learning fine-tuning techniques can be made accessible without enterprise-grade hardware.

**Tech Stack:** Python, PyTorch, Transformers, PEFT
